Chủ đề: Describe a favorite piece of clothing you often wear

PHÁ ĐẢO IELTS SPEAKING PART 2: MÔ TẢ TRANG PHỤC YÊU THÍCH ĐẠT BAND 8.0+! 🚀👕

Bạn đang "đau đầu" với phần IELTS Speaking Part 2, đặc biệt là khi phải mô tả đồ vật? Đừng lo lắng! Hôm nay, chuyên gia IELTS 8.5 sẽ "bật mí" cho bạn một bài mẫu hoàn hảo cùng những từ vựng "đắt giá" để bạn tự tin chinh phục đề bài "Describe a favorite piece of clothing you often wear"! Cùng khám phá ngay! 👇

---

**[SAMPLE ANSWER - Band 8.0+]**

I'd like to talk about my favorite **go-to** item of clothing: a simple, oversized dark blue denim jacket. It's something I reach for almost every day.

I acquired it about three years ago from a vintage store in Da Lat during a spontaneous road trip with friends. It was love at first sight, honestly; it just caught my eye amongst a rack of forgotten garments and I knew I had to have it.

What makes this jacket so special is its incredible versatility. It literally **goes with everything**, from a casual t-shirt and jeans to a summer dress, instantly adding a touch of effortless cool. It's become a **staple** in my wardrobe, almost an extension of my personality.

Beyond its aesthetic appeal, it’s incredibly comfortable. The denim is soft and worn-in, not stiff at all, which makes it perfect for everyday wear. I also love the slightly faded look, which gives it a character that new jackets often lack.

Whenever I put it on, I feel a sense of comfort and confidence. It’s like a warm hug on a chilly evening, and it's accompanied me on countless adventures, from hiking in the mountains to late-night study sessions. It truly holds so many cherished memories.

Even though it's just a jacket, it genuinely **stands the test of time** both in terms of style and durability. It’s definitely one of those items I can’t imagine my closet without, and I plan to keep wearing it for many more years to come. It’s a true **wardrobe essential**.

---

**5 TỪ VỰNG "ĐẮT GIÁ" TỪ BÀI MẪU NÀY BẠN NHẤT ĐỊNH PHẢI BIẾT!**

1.  **Go-to (item/person)** (adj.): Món đồ/người mà bạn luôn lựa chọn, tìm đến vì nó đáng tin cậy hoặc yêu thích nhất.
    *   *Cách dùng:* "This coffee shop is my **go-to** place for morning coffee."
2.  **Staple (in one's wardrobe/diet)** (n.): Món đồ thiết yếu, không thể thiếu, cốt lõi trong một lĩnh vực cụ thể.
    *   *Cách dùng:* "Jeans are a **staple** in most people's wardrobes."
3.  **Goes with everything** (collocation): Hợp với mọi thứ, dễ phối đồ (thường dùng cho quần áo, phụ kiện).
    *   *Cách dùng:* "I love this neutral-colored bag; it **goes with everything**."
4.  **Stand the test of time** (idiom): Đứng vững với thời gian, bền bỉ, không lỗi thời, vẫn còn giá trị sau một thời gian dài.
    *   *Cách dùng:* "His classic songs have **stood the test of time**."
5.  **Wardrobe essential** (collocation): Món đồ thiết yếu trong tủ quần áo, những món đồ cơ bản và cần có.
    *   *Cách dùng:* "A plain white t-shirt is a **wardrobe essential** for everyone."
